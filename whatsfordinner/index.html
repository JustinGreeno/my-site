Life at What's For Dinner

Welcome to What's For Dinner

Located on Route 6 in Pugwash, Nova Scotia, What's For Dinner is your destination for great food, cold drinks, and good times. Whether you're stopping by for lunch, enjoying dinner with family, or catching live music with friends, we've got you covered.

Our menu features pub favorites made with care - from fresh seafood and juicy burgers to crispy wings and homemade comfort food. Full bar with local and imported beers, wines, and craft cocktails.
