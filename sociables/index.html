Life at Sociables

Welcome to Sociables

Located in the heart of downtown Springhill, Nova Scotia, Sociables Pub & Eatery has been serving the community with great food, cold drinks, and a warm, friendly atmosphere. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, enjoying dinner with family, or catching up with friends over drinks, we've got you covered.

Our menu features Canadian pub classics made with care - from our famous fish and chips to hearty shepherd's pie, savory wings, and more. We're vegetarian friendly and always happy to accommodate.
