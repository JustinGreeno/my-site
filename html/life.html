From Farm to City and Back

I grew up on a farm in Nova Scotia, Canada. Those early years taught me the value of hard work, problem-solving, and perseverance. There's something about growing up surrounded by nature that stays with you.

After high school, I moved to British Columbia where I spent 10+ years building a career in counseling, working with some of the most vulnerable populations during the opioid crisis. BC taught me resilience, empathy, and the importance of community.

Recently, I moved back to Nova Scotia to care for my aging mother-in-law. It's been a journey coming home, reconnecting with my roots while pursuing new passions in web development and game design.
