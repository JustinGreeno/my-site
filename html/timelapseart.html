Intro To The Game

Darkness Blooms is a deeply story driven narrative team based RPG. Through the retro style art of 16 bit pixel graphics a story of 6 unlikely young adults are drawn together and given the opportunity to be heroes whether they asked for it or not.

Darkness Blooms is inspired by many classic RPGs namely finally fantasy and zelda mixed with the art style of stardew valley

Darkness Blooms is being solo developed by myself including all the artwork, programming, and narrative storytelling
