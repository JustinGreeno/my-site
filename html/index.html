Welcome!

I'm Justin Greeno, a web developer and digital artist based in Nova Scotia, Canada. I grew up on a farm here in Nova Scotia, spent 10 years in Vancouver, BC working as a counselor on the frontlines of the opioid epidemic, and recently moved back home to care for my aging mother-in-law.

I'm passionate about web development, pixel art and animation, retro video games, gardening, and continuous learning. I'm also solo-developing a retro-style RPG called Darkness Blooms.
