Welcome to Sheryl's

Located in the heart of Pugwash, Nova Scotia, Sheryl's Bakery & Cafe has been serving up homemade goodness with a smile. Our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare fresh breads, famous molasses cookies, and irresistible cinnamon rolls.

Stop in for a hearty lobster roll, warm bowl of chowder, or one of our signature sandwiches. Pair it with a fresh coffee and enjoy the friendly, welcoming atmosphere that keeps locals and visitors coming back.
