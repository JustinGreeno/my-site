Life at Nelly's

Welcome to Nelly's

Located in West Pugwash, Nova Scotia, Nelly's Grill & Dairy Bar has been serving the community with great food, fresh seafood, and friendly service. Whether you're stopping by for fish & chips, a lobster roll, fried chicken, or our famous ice cream, we've got you covered.

Our menu features fresh Atlantic seafood, homemade burgers with local beef, crispy fried chicken, and hand-cut fries. Plus our full dairy bar for dessert!
