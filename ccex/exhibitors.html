Exhibitor Information

Show Your Best at the Fair!

Welcome Exhibitors!

The Cumberland County Exhibition welcomes exhibitors from across the region to showcase their finest livestock, agricultural products, crafts, and more. Whether you're a seasoned exhibitor or participating for the first time, we're here to help make your experience a success.

Entry Deadline

August 15, 2025

All entries must be received by this date. Late entries may not be accepted.

Livestock Exhibitors

Our livestock shows are the heart of the Cumberland County Exhibition. We welcome entries in the following categories:

Categories

  • Beef Cattle (All breeds welcome)
  • Dairy Cattle
  • Horses (Light and Heavy)
  • Sheep
  • Goats
  • Poultry
  • Rabbits

Entry Fees

Animal Type Entry Fee
Cattle (per head) $15.00
Horses (per entry) $10.00
Sheep/Goats (per head) $8.00
Poultry (per bird) $3.00

Health Requirements

All livestock must have current health certificates and vaccinations as required by provincial regulations. Animals will be inspected upon arrival.

4-H Exhibitors

4-H members are the future of agriculture! We're proud to host numerous 4-H competitions and demonstrations.

4-H Events

  • 4-H Beef Achievement Day
  • 4-H Beef Showmanship
  • 4-H Horse Shows (Western & English)
  • 4-H Sheep & Goat Show
  • 4-H Poultry Show
  • 4-H Lifeskills Displays

Note: 4-H entries must be submitted through your local 4-H club leader. Contact your club for specific deadlines and requirements.

Agricultural Hall Exhibits

The Agricultural Hall showcases the best of local produce, baking, preserves, and crafts. All are welcome to enter!

Categories

  • Vegetables & Field Crops
  • Fruits
  • Flowers & Floral Arrangements
  • Baking & Preserves
  • Needlework & Quilting
  • Woodworking & Crafts
  • Photography
  • Youth Division (under 18)

Entry Fees

Entry fee: $2.00 per item (maximum 3 entries per class)

Youth Division: Free

Drop-off Times

All Agricultural Hall exhibits must be delivered to the hall on Friday, August 22nd between 4:00 PM and 8:00 PM. Judging takes place Saturday morning.

Horse Show Exhibitors

Our horse shows feature both English and Western disciplines, with classes for all ages and skill levels.

Show Classes

  • Halter Classes (all ages)
  • Western Pleasure
  • English Pleasure
  • Equitation (Western & English)
  • Barrel Racing
  • Pole Bending
  • Trail Class
  • Lead Line (for young riders)

Stall Reservations: Contact the exhibition office to reserve stalls. Space is limited!
