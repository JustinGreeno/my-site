Events Schedule

Three Days of Country Fun!

Friday - Opening Day

Gates open at 4:00 PM

4:00 PM
Gates Open
Welcome to CCEx! Midway rides begin, food vendors open, and the fair officially kicks off.
5:00 PM
4-H Beef Show
Young farmers showcase their beef cattle in the livestock ring. Come support our 4-H members!
Livestock Ring
6:30 PM
Agricultural Hall Opens
View prize-winning vegetables, baked goods, preserves, quilts, and crafts from local exhibitors.
Exhibition Hall
7:00 PM
Opening Ceremonies
Official welcome with local dignitaries, the singing of O Canada, and recognition of volunteers.
Main Stage
8:00 PM
Live Music: Country Crossroads
Local country band performing classic hits and modern favourites. Dance floor open!
Main Stage

Saturday - Main Day

Gates open at 9:00 AM

9:00 AM
Gates Open - Pancake Breakfast
Start your day with a hearty pancake breakfast served by the Lions Club. All proceeds to charity.
Food Tent
10:00 AM
Horse Show - Halter Classes
Watch beautiful horses compete in halter and conformation classes. All breeds welcome.
Horse Ring
11:00 AM
4-H Sheep & Goat Show
4-H members show their sheep and goats in showmanship and breed classes.
Livestock Ring
12:00 PM
Petting Zoo Opens
Bring the kids to meet friendly farm animals including baby goats, lambs, chicks, and bunnies!
Petting Zoo Area
1:00 PM
Tractor Pull - Antique Class
Vintage tractors compete in this crowd-favourite pulling competition. Great photo opportunities!
Pull Track
2:30 PM
Heavy Horse Pull
Powerful draft horses showcase their strength in the traditional horse pull competition.
Pull Track
4:00 PM
Poultry Show Awards
Winners announced for best chickens, ducks, turkeys, and other poultry. View exhibits all day.
Poultry Barn
7:00 PM
Demolition Derby
The main event! Watch cars crash, smash, and bash in this thrilling demolition derby. Reserved grandstand seating available.
Arena - Ticketed Event

Sunday - Family Day

Gates open at 10:00 AM

10:00 AM
Church Service
Non-denominational outdoor church service. All welcome. Bring a lawn chair.
Main Stage Area
11:00 AM
4-H Achievement Day
Grand Champion selections and awards for all 4-H livestock classes. Celebrate our young farmers!
Livestock Ring
12:00 PM
Kids' Pedal Tractor Pull
Children ages 4-10 compete in this adorable pedal tractor pulling contest. Prizes for all participants!
Pull Track
1:30 PM
Beef Cattle Show - Open Classes
Open class beef cattle competition featuring the region's finest herds.
Livestock Ring
3:00 PM
Supreme Champion Selection
The best of the best compete for the title of Supreme Champion Livestock.
Livestock Ring
4:00 PM
Livestock Auction
4-H and open class animals go up for auction. Support our young farmers!
Auction Ring
6:00 PM
Closing Ceremonies & Fireworks
Thank you for attending CCEx! The fair closes with a spectacular fireworks display.
Main Grounds

Important Information

Schedule subject to change. Some events may require separate admission. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult on the midway. No outside food or beverages permitted.
