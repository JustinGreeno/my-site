4-H Youth Programs

The Cumberland County Exhibition is proud to host 4-H competitions and demonstrations throughout the fair. Watch young farmers showcase their animals and skills in:

Beef Showmanship

See 4-H members present their cattle with pride and skill.

Horse Shows

Western pleasure, equitation, and more competitive events.

Small Animals

Rabbits, poultry, and other small animals on display.

Interested in 4-H? Visit our information booth to learn how your child can get involved!

Parent & Guardian Information

For your child's safety, please supervise children at all times, especially around animals and rides. Hand washing stations are available throughout the grounds. Lost children should be reported to the main office immediately.
