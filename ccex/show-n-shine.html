Kids Show 'N Shine 2025

Registration Form

Register Your Little Car Enthusiast!

Welcome to the CCEx Kids Show 'N Shine! This is a fun event for children to show off their favorite toy vehicles, ride-on cars, decorated wagons, or bicycles.

Who Can Participate?

This event is for children 9 years and younger.

If your child does not own a vehicle, they can still participate as a judge and will still receive a treat bag!
