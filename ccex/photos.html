4-H in Action

Our 4-H members work year-round to raise and train their animals. The fair is their chance to shine and show what they've learned about responsibility, animal husbandry, and agricultural skills.

Showmanship

Judged on how well they present and handle their animals in the ring.

Conformation

Animals judged on breed standards and quality.

Achievement

Overall awards recognizing hard work and dedication.
