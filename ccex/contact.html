Frequently Asked Questions

When is the fair?

The Cumberland County Exhibition is held annually in September. Check our home page for exact dates.

How much is admission?

Adults: $10 | Children (6-12): $5 | Under 6: Free. Family passes and multi-day passes are available at the gate.

Can I bring my dog?

For the safety of our livestock, only service animals are permitted on the fairgrounds.

Is there accessible parking?

Yes, accessible parking is available near the main entrance. Our grounds are mostly accessible, though some areas may have uneven surfaces.

How do I enter livestock?

Entry forms are available on our website closer to fair time. Contact our livestock director for more information.
