The People Behind the Fair

Meet Our Volunteers

The Cumberland County Exhibition is made possible by dedicated volunteers who give their time and energy to keep this tradition alive. Our board members represent diverse backgrounds in agriculture, business, and community service, united by their love for the fair.

Click on any member to learn more about them!

Executive Committee

🧑
Robert MacDonald
President

Robert has been involved with the CCEx for over 25 years, starting as a 4-H member showing beef cattle. A third-generation farmer from Pugwash, he brings a deep understanding of agriculture and a passion for preserving rural traditions.
