Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fair hours?

The fair is open Friday 5PM-10PM, Saturday 10AM-10PM, Sunday 10AM-6PM, and Monday 10AM-4PM. Vendors are expected to be open during all operating hours.

When can I set up my booth?

Vendor setup is available starting Thursday afternoon from 2PM-8PM, and Friday morning from 8AM-4PM. All booths must be ready before the fair opens Friday at 5PM.

Is electrical power included?

Electrical hookups are available for an additional $50 per weekend. Please indicate your power needs on your application (standard 120V or 240V).

Can I sell food without a food truck?

Pre-packaged foods and baked goods may be sold from craft booths with proper labeling. All other food sales require a food vendor space and health inspection certificate.

What happens if it rains?

Outdoor vendors should have their own tent/canopy. The fair operates rain or shine. Indoor craft vendors are protected from weather.
